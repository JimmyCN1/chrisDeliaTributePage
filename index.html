Chris D'Elia

One of the funniest people alive

Here is a timeline of Chris D'Elia's life:

  • 1980 - Born in Montclair, New Jersey
  • 1998 - Graduated from La Canada High School
  • 2006 - Started his stand-up comedy career
  • 2006 - Married actress Emily Montague
  • 2010 - Divorced from Emily Montague
  • 2012 - Began hosting the Ten Minute Podcast with his friends Bryan Callen and Will Sasso
  • 2013 - Released his debut parody album as MC "Chank Smith", called Such is Life
  • 2013 - Released his first one hour stand-up special, White Male, Black Comic
  • 2014 - Began a lead role in the NBC sitcom, Undateable
  • 2015 - Released his second special, Incorrigible
  • 2017 - Release his third special, Man on Fire
  • 2017 - Began hosting his weekly podcast titled Congratulations with Chris D'Elia, where he jokingly refer's to his listeners as his "Babies"
  • 2018 - Started touring for his fourth special, Follow the Leader
  • 2019 - He announced that he would be playing a recurring role in the second season of the Netflix thriller series You
